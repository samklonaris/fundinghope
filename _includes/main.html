Funding Hope is a private community dedicated to increasing the number of researchers working on endometriosis treatments.
Endometriosis affects 11% of women and takes 7 years on average to be diagnosed.
Endometriosis research only receives .03% of the medical research funding supplied by the National Institutes of Health
